# Assignment-3 Part-E — Final Project Report

## Submission Requirements

- **Who submits:** **Every student individually**
- **File naming:** `Assignment-3-PartE-<StudentName>-<StudentID>.doc/pdf`
- **Example:** `Assignment-3-PartE-NabinSharma-12345678.pdf`

## What to Include

The final project report should cover:

1. **Title Page** — Project title, team members (names and student IDs)
2. **Abstract**
3. **Introduction** — problem statement and motivation
4. **Related Work** — relevant literature review
5. **Dataset** — description, preprocessing steps, train/val/test splits
6. **Methodology** — model architecture, training procedure, hyperparameters
7. **Results** — quantitative and qualitative evaluation
8. **Discussion** — analysis of results, limitations, future work
9. **Conclusion**
10. **References**
11. **Appendices**
    - **A. Individual Contribution** — each team member's contribution percentage (must be agreed upon by all members and signed)

## Notes on Individual Contribution Section

- Every member submitting the report must include their personal contribution details in **Appendices: A. Individual Contribution**.
- All team members must add their contribution percentage in the table and sign it after discussion.
- If team members cannot agree on contributions, each can submit with their own perspective. The subject coordinator will investigate and make a final decision.
- The content and results sections can be the same for all team members' submissions.
